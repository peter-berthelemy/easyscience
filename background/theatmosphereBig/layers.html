The layers of the atmosphere are defined by change in temperature. This comes about because of different chemical makeups etc

Troposphere: Full of weather

Stratosphere: Full of planes

Mesosphere: Full of nothing

Ionosphere: Full of electricity

Magnetosphere: Full of solar stuff
